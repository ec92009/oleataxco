Founder perspective

Built around long-term thinking

I believe tax strategy should be intentional, integrated, and tailored to each client.

I'm a licensed CPA with nearly a decade of experience advising entrepreneurs, multi-entity businesses, and growing founders. Before Olea Tax Co., I spent years inside tax departments and advisory environments supporting complex client situations.

Over time, I saw how messy workflows, reactive deadlines, and fragmented communication often distracted from what clients actually needed: clarity, structure, and thoughtful strategy.

I founded Olea Tax Co. to work differently. I work directly with each client to reduce tax exposure, improve entity structure, and ensure business decisions are made intentionally — not rushed at year-end.
